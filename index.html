Buri Khalifa

Burj Khalifa is the world's tallest structure. With a total height of 829.8 m and more than 160 stories.The Burj Khalifa has been the tallest structure and building in the world since its topping out in 2009.

Buri Al Arab

The Burj Al Arab is a luxury hotel in Dubai, United Arab Emirates.Burj Al Arab stands on an artificial island that is 280 m (920 ft) from Jumeirah Beach and is connected to the mainland by a private curving bridge.

Dubai Aquarium

Dubai Aquarium and Underwater Zoo, contains more than 33,000 aquatic animals and the largest collection of sand tiger sharks anywhere. There are numerous ways for visitors to experience the main Aquarium tank.

Museum of the Future

Museum of the Future is a landmark devoted to innovative and futuristic ideologies.Located in the Financial District of Dubai, this museum is a torus-shaped building with windows in the form of a poem about the future.

Dubai Expo 2020

Inspiration found a new home at Expo 2020 Dubai over 182 days of collaboration, innovation and the marvels that humanity can accomplish together. This mega-fair proved to be an unmissable experience narrating the pivotal story of things to come.

Dubai Frame

The Dubai Frame an observatory, museum, and monument in Zabeel Park, Dubai.It holds the record for the largest frame in the world.The building mainly serves as an observatory, providing views of old Dubai in the north and newer parts in the south.

Aquaventure Waterpark

For waterpark fans Aquaventure is an absolute must-see Dubai attraction the largest waterpark in the world, it's packed with 105 different slides and rides including some of the highest and fastest in the world.

Dubai Miracle Garden

A world of floral wonder awaits you at Dubai Miracle Garden, the world's largest natural flower garden. The 72,000sqm park has an impressive line-up of famous buildings and structures completely transformed into colourful flower exhibits.
